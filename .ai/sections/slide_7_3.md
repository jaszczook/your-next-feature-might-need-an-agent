# SLIDE 7.3 — THANKS

---

## Visual

One word, centered, large:

> Thanks.

With a period — not an exclamation mark, not a question mark. No "Questions?" prompt on the slide.

*Optional:* small contact info underneath, dim — only if bank culture expects it.

---

## Spoken

*Pick the one that fits the room, in order of preference:*

1. "Thanks. I'd love to hear what you build."
2. "Thanks. Let's talk."
3. "Thanks. Happy to take questions, or grab me later in Slack."

## Delivery

Match the colleague-to-colleague register the whole talk has been in. The shortest version is almost always the right one. Do not put "any questions?" on the slide — it tanks the closing energy.

**Duration:** ~10 seconds.
