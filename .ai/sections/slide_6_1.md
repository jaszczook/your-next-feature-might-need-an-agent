# SLIDE 6.1 — DEMO VIDEO

---

## Visual

The video itself, full-bleed or as large as slide tooling allows. No title bar, no logo, no caption. Black bars around the video are fine if the aspect ratio doesn't match — anything else on the slide pulls focus.

- **Video length:** 60–90 seconds
- **Embedded, not linked**
- **Do not autoplay** — speaker starts it manually after the spoken intro. Autoplay on slide entry is a liability for a live demo.

## Production Notes *(add video later)*

Cut to moments that show pattern-recognizable behavior:

1. A tool call into an attached file (P1)
2. The agent producing a draft, with research informing it (P2)
3. A moment of state continuity — e.g., "I remember you said earlier..." (P4)
4. A guardrail moment if one exists (P3)

Aim for ~4 moments in ~90 seconds.

**Redact** any confidential client data or internal system names visible in the UI. Use obviously-fake placeholder content.

---

## Spoken

*Advance to this slide; video is paused/idle. Deliver the intro (~20 seconds), then start the video manually.*

"Let me show you one.

This is an agent we're building at this bank. I work on it. It accompanies people when they're authoring financial documents — drafts sections, pulls research from attached files, keeps track of what's been written and what's next.

I'm going to shut up for about a minute and a half and let you watch. See if you can spot the patterns we just walked through."

[start video — stay silent]

[video ends — hold silence 2–3 seconds, look at the room]

"That's the one I get to work on.

[beat]

Every pattern I showed you is in there somewhere. I'll let you find them."

*Then advance to the closing.*

## Delivery

Land the intro quiet — no curtain-raise energy. Start the video promptly; don't let the silence stretch before it. During the video: **completely silent.** After: hold the silence before speaking.

**Duration:** ~2 minutes total (including the video).
